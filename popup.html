ABOUT

Grandma is an AI based application that automatically detects violent/hate speech from all websites. It either warns you to not visit the page, or automatically blurs it for you. Thus, protecting both adults and especially children from witnessing any propaganda or violence that can later affect their decisions.

QUESTIONS

Here are some questions that can help you understand our purpose better.

Do people need us?

Based on reasearch, 91% of people witness hate speech, while 1/3 of people are themselves the victim. The majority of people have searched for such applications that can detect hate speech and failed to find one (well, until now).

What services do we provide?

We offer two services: One is for individuals who want to protect themselves or their children against witnessing any violence or propaganda. Second is for the police and research organizations interested in finding the sources of hate speech.

Who are we?

We are a team of five energetic and enthusiastic individuals, who have one common goal - make the world a somewhat better and kinder place!
